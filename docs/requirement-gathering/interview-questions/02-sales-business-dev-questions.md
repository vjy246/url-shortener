# URL Shortener Service - Sales/Business Development Interview Questions

## Overview
This document contains preliminary interview questions for Sales and Business Development teams. These questions focus on go-to-market strategy, pricing, customer segments, competitive positioning, and sales channels.

---

## 1. Customer & Market Segments

### 1.1 Target Customer Profiles
- **Q1.1.1**: Who are the ideal customers? (enterprises, SMBs, individual creators, agencies)
- **Q1.1.2**: What industries are we targeting? (tech, marketing, media, finance, healthcare, etc.)
- **Q1.1.3**: What customer size/revenue ranges are we targeting? (startup, mid-market, enterprise)
- **Q1.1.4**: Geographic focus? (US only, EU, APAC, global)
- **Q1.1.5**: What's the estimated TAM (Total Addressable Market)?

### 1.2 Customer Personas
- **Q1.2.1**: What roles/titles are key decision-makers? (CMO, Head of Product, Head of Engineering, VP Marketing)
- **Q1.2.2**: Who are the end users vs. decision-makers vs. influencers?
- **Q1.2.3**: What are their pain points with current solutions?
- **Q1.2.4**: What are their success criteria?
- **Q1.2.5**: What's their budget allocation for this type of solution?

---

## 2. Competitive Positioning

### 2.1 Competitive Landscape
- **Q2.1.1**: Who are the main competitors? (bit.ly, Rebrandly, custom in-house solutions, etc.)
- **Q2.1.2**: What are their pricing models?
- **Q2.1.3**: What's our competitive advantage?
- **Q2.1.4**: What are our weaknesses vs. competitors?
- **Q2.1.5**: What features do competitors have that we must/should have?

### 2.2 Positioning Strategy
- **Q2.2.1**: What's our unique value proposition?
- **Q2.2.2**: What's the primary buyer motivation? (cost, ease-of-use, features, support, compliance, security)
- **Q2.2.3**: How will we position against free tools vs. paid solutions?
- **Q2.2.4**: What's our messaging for different segments? (SMB vs. Enterprise)
- **Q2.2.5**: Do we have differentiated messaging for different industries?

### 2.3 Market Positioning
- **Q2.3.1**: Are we a premium, mid-market, or value player?
- **Q2.3.2**: What's our market positioning vs. big players like bit.ly?
- **Q2.3.3**: Are there underserved market segments we can own?
- **Q2.3.4**: What's our long-term competitive strategy?

---

## 3. Go-To-Market Strategy

### 3.1 Launch Strategy
- **Q3.1.1**: What's the launch approach? (stealth beta, limited beta, public, partner-driven)
- **Q3.1.2**: Who are launch partners or beta customers?
- **Q3.1.3**: What's the timeline for different market segments?
- **Q3.1.4**: Do we have early customer commitments or LOIs (Letters of Intent)?
- **Q3.1.5**: What's the beta feedback incorporation plan?

### 3.2 Sales Channels
- **Q3.2.1**: Primary sales channel? (direct sales, self-serve, partnerships, resellers, agencies)
- **Q3.2.2**: Expected sales cycle length? (self-serve, SMB, enterprise)
- **Q3.2.3**: Do we have sales team in place? (hiring needs, structure)
- **Q3.2.4**: Will we use sales enablement tools? (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- **Q3.2.5**: Do we have product-led growth (PLG) strategy? (freemium → upsell)

### 3.3 Customer Acquisition
- **Q3.3.1**: What's the expected CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)?
- **Q3.3.2**: What's the expected payback period?
- **Q3.3.3**: What acquisition channels are planned? (ads, content, partnerships, referrals, community)
- **Q3.3.4**: Who owns marketing, sales, and customer success?
- **Q3.3.5**: Marketing budget? (% of revenue, absolute amount)

---

## 4. Pricing & Monetization

### 4.1 Pricing Model
- **Q4.1.1**: Pricing structure? (freemium, tiered SaaS, usage-based, hybrid)
- **Q4.1.2**: Pricing tiers and features at each tier?
- **Q4.1.3**: Expected price point for different segments?
- **Q4.1.4**: Free trial duration? (14 days, 30 days, freemium)
- **Q4.1.5**: Annual vs. monthly billing preferred by customers?

### 4.2 Revenue Expectations
- **Q4.2.1**: Year 1 revenue target?
- **Q4.2.2**: Customer acquisition targets? (# customers, revenue)
- **Q4.2.3**: Expected churn rate? (% monthly, annual)
- **Q4.2.4**: Expected ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)? (by segment)
- **Q4.2.5**: Expected net retention rate? (expansion revenue, downgrades)

### 4.3 Monetization Options
- **Q4.3.1**: API rate limits and pricing? (per 1000 calls, monthly limits)
- **Q4.3.2**: Premium features monetization? (custom domains, advanced analytics, teams)
- **Q4.3.3**: Enterprise custom pricing? (commitment discounts, volume discounts)
- **Q4.3.4**: Partner/white-label revenue models?
- **Q4.3.5**: Seat-based vs. usage-based pricing for teams?

## 5. Sales Enablement

### 5.1 Sales Tools & Process
- **Q5.1.1**: What sales collateral is needed? (one-pagers, case studies, ROI calculators, comparison charts)
- **Q5.1.2**: Demo environments or trial accounts needed?
- **Q5.1.3**: Comparison documents vs. competitors?
- **Q5.1.4**: Sales playbooks for different segments? (SMB, Enterprise)
- **Q5.1.5**: Sales training program needed?

### 5.2 Sales Process
- **Q5.2.1**: What's the sales qualification process? (BANT, MEDDIC, other)
- **Q5.2.2**: Deal size expectations? (small, medium, large deals)
- **Q5.2.3**: Sales cycle timeline? (from lead to closed deal)
- **Q5.2.4**: Contract terms? (month-to-month, annual, multi-year)
- **Q5.2.5**: Legal/compliance requirements for enterprise sales?

### 5.3 Customer Success
- **Q5.3.1**: Expected customer support model? (chat, email, phone, documentation, dedicated CSM)
- **Q5.3.2**: SLAs for support? (response time, resolution time)
- **Q5.3.3**: Onboarding process for new customers?
- **Q5.3.4**: Customer success metrics? (adoption, feature usage, satisfaction, retention)
- **Q5.3.5**: Expansion/upsell strategy? (when, what, how)

## 6. Market Insights

### 6.1 Market Trends
- **Q6.1.1**: What market trends are driving demand? (social commerce, omnichannel marketing, QR codes, etc.)
- **Q6.1.2**: Are there seasonal variations in demand? (peak seasons, off-seasons)
- **Q6.1.3**: What's the current market growth rate?
- **Q6.1.4**: Emerging technologies affecting market? (AI, web3, etc.)

### 6.2 Customer Feedback
- **Q6.2.1**: What customer feedback have you heard?
- **Q6.2.2**: What are the most requested features?
- **Q6.2.3**: Why do customers churn?
- **Q6.2.4**: What's the NPS (Net Promoter Score) expectation?
- **Q6.2.5**: What are deal-breakers for potential customers?

### 6.3 Sales Velocity
- **Q6.3.1**: Expected conversion rates? (lead to trial, trial to customer)
- **Q6.3.2**: Average sales cycle length by segment?
- **Q6.3.3**: Deal closure rate? (% of qualified leads that close)
- **Q6.3.4**: Win rate vs. competitors?

## 8. Sales Targets & Metrics

### 8.1 Key Sales Metrics
- **Q8.1.1**: Customer acquisition target by segment? (# customers per month)
- **Q8.1.2**: Revenue target by quarter/year?
- **Q8.1.3**: Win rate expectations? (vs. no-decision, vs. competitors)
- **Q8.1.4**: Average deal size? (by segment)
- **Q8.1.5**: Customer lifetime value (LTV)?

### 8.2 Sales Efficiency Metrics
- **Q8.2.1**: LTV:CAC ratio target? (3:1, 5:1, higher)
- **Q8.2.2**: Sales quota expectations?
- **Q8.2.3**: Sales productivity? (revenue per sales rep)
- **Q8.2.4**: Lead-to-customer conversion rate target?
- **Q8.2.5**: Time-to-revenue metric?

### 8.3 Reporting Requirements
- **Q8.3.1**: Sales pipeline reporting cadence? (weekly, monthly)
- **Q8.3.2**: Forecast accuracy expectations?
- **Q8.3.3**: Sales metrics dashboard? (pipeline, velocity, win-loss analysis)
- **Q8.3.4**: Customer success metrics dashboard?

- **Q9.1.2**: Customer acquisition challenges?

## Notes
- Validate market assumptions with customer research/interviews
- Define clear customer personas and buyer personas
- Establish pricing based on value, not just cost
- Build sales process aligned with customer buying journey
- Plan for partner/channel enablement early
